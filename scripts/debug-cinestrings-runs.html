CineStrings - Runs

Orchestral Strings

Recorded at the MGM Scoring Stage at Sony Pictures Studios in Los Angeles, CineStrings Runs is an essential addition to the CineSymphony series. The library features a range of performed runs in different scales, directions, and lengths, as well as other colorful string textures and effects to expand your template.
