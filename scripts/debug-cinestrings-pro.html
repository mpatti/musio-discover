CineStrings - Pro

Orchestral Strings

Recorded at the MGM Scoring Stage at Sony Pictures Studios in Los Angeles, CineStrings PRO is the final installment of the CineStrings series. The library features a range of new articulations for both ensemble and individual string sections, including appassionata, con sordino, and more. Also included traditional articulations such as legato, sustain, shorts, and FX.
