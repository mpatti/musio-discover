LA Modern Percussion

Iconic Blockbuster Percussion

LA Modern Percussion is the definitive blockbuster percussion collection. Recorded & mixed by mixing legend Alan Meyerson at Warner Bros Eastwood Stage, the drums were produced exactly the way they are on the high budget film score projects he works on. LA Modern Percussion was designed to be sculpted, processed, & layered into larger than life, fully produced drum ensembles.
